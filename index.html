About Mother's Day



Mother's Day is a celebration honoring the mother of the family or individual, as well as motherhood, maternal bonds, and the influence of mothers in society. It is celebrated on different days in many parts of the world, most commonly in the months of March or May.Mother's Day is a special day celebrated across the world. On this day, we try to express our love,respect,honor and gratitude for our mother.No other gift can be more special to a mother than her children's love and respect.
